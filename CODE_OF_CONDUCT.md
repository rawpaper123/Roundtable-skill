# Code of Conduct

Roundtable Skill is for people building safer agentic coding workflows. Keep
project spaces useful, respectful, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different experience levels, tools, languages, and working
  styles.
- Keep feedback specific, actionable, and relevant to Roundtable Skill.
- Assume good intent, but accept corrections when your impact is harmful.
- Do not post secrets, private runtime data, mailbox contents, tokens, or other
  sensitive information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Publicly sharing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic promotion, spam, or deliberately disruptive behavior.
- Encouraging unsafe handling of secrets, credentials, private agent mail, or
  runtime data.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, block users, or take
other reasonable action to keep the project safe and useful.

Report conduct concerns through GitHub issues only if no private information is
involved. If a report includes private or sensitive information, contact a
maintainer privately instead.
